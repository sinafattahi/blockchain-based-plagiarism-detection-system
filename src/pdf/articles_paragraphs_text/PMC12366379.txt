Following publication of the original article it was reported that there was an error in the first paragraph of the Results section. The corrected text is given in bold.

The original sentences were: The operative conditions, hospitalization metrics, and postoperative complications were compared between the two groups. The operative duration in TC Group was longer than that in TD Group; however, this difference was not statistical (P> 0.05). Additionally, TC Group experienced less intraoperative blood loss compared to TD Group, with no statistically significant difference (P> 0.05).

The corrected sentences are: The operative conditions, hospitalization metrics, and postoperative complications were compared between the two groups.The operative duration in TC Group was shorter than that in TD Group, this difference was with statistical significance (P< 0.05). Additionally, TC Group experienced more intraoperative blood loss compared to TD Group, with no statistically significant difference (P> 0.05).
